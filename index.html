A B O U T

IES Symposium 2025

The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Symposium is a premier technical event dedicated to fostering innovation, knowledge sharing, and skill development in the field of industrial electronics. Funded by the IEEE IES Student and Chapter Leadership Activity (SCLA) program, this symposium brings together students, professionals, and researchers through a rich blend of activities. The event features hands-on workshops, a hackathon focused on real-world industrial challenges, and a Leaders Meet that facilitates interaction between industry leaders, researchers, and IEEE IES professionals. It also includes a two-day immersive Technical Training Program covering tools and technologies in industrial electronics, along with an industry-Academia Conclave that promotes meaningful dialogue and collaboration between students and professionals. The symposium aims to inspire and equip participants to contribute actively to the advancement of industrial electronics.

O U R  H O S T

NIT CALICUT

National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC) is one of India’s premier institutions for technical education and research, recognized for its academic excellence and vibrant campus environment. Located in Kerala, NIT Calicut is known for fostering innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and leadership among students. With a strong focus on cutting-edge research and industry engagement, NITC provides an ideal setting for hosting high-impact events like the IEEE IES Symposium. The institute’s commitment to technological advancement and its active IEEE student community make it a fitting host for a gathering of experts, researchers, and students in the field of industrial electronics.
