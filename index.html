IES Symposium 2025

The IET Industrial Electronics Society (IES) symposium is a premier school-level event dedicated to fostering innovation, knowledge sharing, and collaboration among students and professionals. Funded by the IET Students and Chapter Sustainability Activity (SCSA) program, this symposium brings together students, professionals, and researchers in the field of Industrial Electronics from across India. Through insightful paper presentations, keynote lectures, hands-on workshops, and technical talks, the symposium fosters innovation and skill development in critical fields like process automation, industrial drives, robotics, and embedded systems. The event also includes an interaction session between industry mentors and participants, bridging the gap between academia and industry. With a strong emphasis on knowledge exchange and skill-building, the symposium helps participants explore emerging trends in industrial electronics, while enhancing their technical and professional skills. By aligning with industry demands and academic excellence, the symposium aims to develop confident and competent engineers who contribute actively to the advancement of industrial electronics.
